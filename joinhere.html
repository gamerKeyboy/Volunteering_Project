Join here

Requirements:

  • interest in marine science
  • you like to speak English
  • you like water and animals
  • Minimum age: 18 years or younger if you are accompanied by an adult
  • -> Become a volunteer with us!
Benefits:
  • free accommodation and travel for you
  • learn to surf and dive for free
  • get to know people from all over the world
  • lots of information about the underwater world and the animals in the sea

Your day with us from Monday to Friday:

Your working day starts at 9 a.m. at the science station. Your lunch break is from 12 p.m. to 1 p.m. You can spend the lunch in the station or in the guest house. Your working day ends around 4 p.m. Every Wednesday there is a meeting at 5 p.m.followed by a barbecue with the team.

Highlights:

Barcelona is a very beautiful and popular city in Europe. It is a city full of life and cultural diversity. Barcelona is known for its impressive architecture. The Sagrada Família, a famous basilica, is the most popular attraction in Barcelona. Discover historic streets, great markets and beautiful cafes in Barcelona. You can see all these things! You go on a trip to Barcelona with all the volunteers. A tour guide will show you the city of Barcelona. Enjoy this special day!

Free Time:

You have many options for activities in your free time. Here are some examples:

  • surf and learn to surf
  • scuba diving and snorkeling in the sea
  • play beach volleyball with friends
  • swim and meet other volunteers on the beach
  • enjoy your free time shopping or walking in our town
