your duties

The coast of Barcelona is home to many marine animals. This natural habitat gives the animals food and protection. This habitat is endangered by climate change and other factors . As a motivated volunteer, you can discover the underwater world of Barcelona and support the scientists in their work.

  • observe and count animals
  • study animals in the laboratory and in the sea
  • breed new corals with the scientists
  • photograph fish and corals
  • record data in the computer
  • pick up the garbage on the beach
  • take water samples
