Accommodation

You will stay in our guest house with 10 rooms. The house has 2 floors. On each floor there is a bathroom with two showers and a big kitchen. In the kitchen you can cook and meet other volunteers.

The highlight is the roof terrace. There you can relax after work and enjoy the evening.

Enjoy your time!
