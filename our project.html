Our Project

The organisation and the station were founded in Barcelona in 2015. It is a team of international employees. The project is supported by the European Union because the Spanish government has no financial resources. Therefore, volunteers are important to support the projects.

The goal of the diving and marine conservation project is to preserve and protect the marine ecosystem. In addition, the biological diversity of the underwater world should be preserved. The scientists want to teach young people about protecting the sea.

We work with modern technology on land and underwater. The scientists record all the data in the computer and compare the data. These records are important for the future.

Corals are most affected by climate change and that's why we breed new corals. As a volunteer, you help us.
